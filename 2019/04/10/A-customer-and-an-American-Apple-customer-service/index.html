A customer and an American Apple customer service

Posted on 2019-04-10 Edited on 2019-09-03

惊闻税率降低，14天内买的苹果产品可以拨打400官方电话退差价

Google电话号码、拨打转普通话服务，一气呵成

“对不起，现在电话量过于繁多，请您稍后再尝试” * 5

我亲你猫 我吃柠檬

灵光一闪，重拨电话、自信选择English line

英文线就人性化很多啦： “During the waiting, you can choose to listen beautiful music provided by Apple Music, press 1 for pop, 2 for rock, 3 for jazz, if you prefer to wait in silence, press 4…”

牛逼，还可以点歌的，就是可惜没有郭德纲相声大全

那就Jazz吧，感觉自己是全曹西最靓的尼哥

主歌都没听完就接通了。

Apple Support (referred as to ‘Apple’): “Apple support, what can I do for you?”
Me: “Hello, can you speak Chinese?”
Apple: “…”
Me: “…”
Apple: “Sorry, I am an American and I haven’t yet mastered Chinese.”

[disturbing silence]

Apple: “May I put you through Mandarin line?”
Me: “Guess why I’m calling you?”

[disturbing silence]

Me: “Fine, I’ll speak in English anyway. I’m calling to ask for help in tax-drawback for my newly-bought AirPods.”
Apple: “Sorry, what?”
Me: “I mean, since the tax rate of Chinese has dropped, the newly bought Apple products can enjoy a tax-refund service by 400-hotline-bling.”
Apple: “Wow”
Me: “Wow”

[disturbing silence]

Apple: “But I’m afraid I can’t retrieve your information through my backend…”
Me: “Because they are separate systems?”
Apple: “Because they are separate systems.”
Me: “Maybe I will try to get through that Mandarin line later.”
Apple: [With firm voice] “No, I will handle this for you.”
Me: “Wow”
Apple: “Wow”
Me: “So your plan?”
Apple: “You can provide me with your name, e-mail address and phone number.”
Me: “And then?”
Apple: “I will send an e-mail for you.”
Me: “That’s all?”
Apple: “That’s all.”

[disturbing silence]

Me: “Fine. My name is Jianing Shi”
Apple: “Gianin Chi”
Me: “No, it’s J-i-a-n-i-n-g S-h-i”
Apple: “Gianin Shi”

[disturbing silence]

Apple: “Sorry but your phone signal is poor.”
Me: “Yes, thanks to my latest model of iPhone.”
Apple: “That’s pretty understandable.”

[disturbing silence]

Apple: “So, tell me your name again.”
Me: … J-i-a-n-i-n-g S-h-i, and my phone number is …
Apple: “Got it. I will send an e-mail to you later, and have a nic…”

[phone signal lost]

(完)
